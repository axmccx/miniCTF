Welcome to the Hack Lab's Capture the Flag workshop. You will get access to a network of vulnerable servers. Each server contains a flag.txt file, only readable by the root user. Use your hacking skills to penatrate the servers to obtain access to the flag contents to score points!

Requirements

Virtualbox running Kali Linux. Two versions will be provided, a standard and a light version. The light version is recommended for less powerfull computers.

Getting Started

Teams can be 1-3 people. Register your team's account. Visit the profile page to obtain the vpn config file and configuration instructions. Submit the contents of the flags at the Challenges page.

Now go hack all the vulnerable servers!

Feeling lost? Post your question on the forum.
